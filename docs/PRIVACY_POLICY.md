# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: December 31, 2024

## Introduction

Gear AI CoPilot ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web services (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address, name, profile photo
- **Vehicle Information**: VIN, make, model, year, photos, maintenance records
- **Payment Information**: Credit card details (processed by Stripe, not stored by us)
- **Chat History**: Conversations with our AI assistant
- **Support Communications**: Messages sent to customer support

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: Pages viewed, features used, time spent in app
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Location Data**: Approximate location (only if you grant permission)
- **Diagnostic Data**: OBD-II data from your vehicle (only with explicit consent)
- **Analytics Data**: App performance, crashes, errors

### Third-Party Data

- **VIN Decoding**: We use NHTSA vPIC API to decode VINs (public data)
- **Vehicle Valuation**: Market data from third-party APIs (Phase 3+)
- **Owner's Manuals**: Publicly available manufacturer documentation

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide and maintain the Service
- Create and manage your account
- Process transactions and subscriptions
- Provide AI-powered automotive assistance
- Send service updates and notifications
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
- Comply with legal obligations
- Communicate with you about your account

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location

- **Database**: Supabase (AWS-hosted, encrypted at rest)
- **File Storage**: Supabase Storage (photos, documents)
- **Authentication**: Firebase Auth (Google Cloud)

### Security Measures

- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Industry-standard TLS/SSL for data in transit
- Row-Level Security (RLS) on database
- Regular security audits
- Limited employee access to user data
- Two-factor authentication for admin accounts

### Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Backups**: Retained for 90 days, then permanently deleted
- **Legal Requirements**: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
